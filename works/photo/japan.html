Japan

July 2016

After the research project over the summer I took a quick trip to Japan before heading off back to the United States. I've been to Japan before, but this time around my friends and I focused on food and experiences, the best of which was climbing Mt. Fuji. It was a hell of a trip, and the climb itself was very difficult for me. Very much worth it for the sunrise though.
