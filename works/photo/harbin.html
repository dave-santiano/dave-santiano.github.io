Harbin, China

February 2014

Harbin is known for its famous ice festival, where chunks of ice are meticulously carved to create sculpture and castles to show off their skills. The ice structures are then lit up with lights so that during the night they take on a completely different look and feel. The city of Harbin also has some of the best food I have ever eaten, with sugar coated sweet potatoes and hot pot that will knock your socks off. Try to visit when you get a chance, especially with the winter Olympics coming up in a bit!
