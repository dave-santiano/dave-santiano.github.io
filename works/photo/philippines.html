The Philippines

July 2013

All of my immediate family is from the Philippines, so the summer after graduation we decided to hop on over there before I went off to start university. When I took these photos I tried to do an exercise in quality over quantity, and I think I've achieved a bit of quality here. The colors and vibrance of the Philippines have always been something that I have wanted to capture through my photography, and until that point, I didn't find the opportunity to do so. So take a look and see what we have to offer!
