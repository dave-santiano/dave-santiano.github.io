Celebrate Women

March 2016

“Arduino empowers me because it turns my creativity into reality, and because thinking as a maker makes me feel like everything is in my control.” – Prudence Yao

CELEBRATE WOMEN is a photo series that was done for International Women's day on March 8th. The creative lead and creator of the idea was Nicole Chan, who does extraordinary social activism work with her photography. What we did was open up a sign-up sheet and schedule people who identify as women for a photoshoot with them and something that empowers them. We asked them to write us a statement on why the object inspired them and placed it as the caption underneath the photo along with a "#CELEBRATEWOMEN". For a bit more info, check out the Facebook event page here.
