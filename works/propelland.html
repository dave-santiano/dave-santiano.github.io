Vitro Samples e-commerce platform

Lead a project to help Vitro Architectural Glass update their Samples Shopify website. The project primarily focused on providing front-end updates to enhance their back-office reporting and data analysis capabilities. We did this by enhancing customer identification during the checkout process, and establishing new rules and restrictions based on different types of customers. For the back-office reports we wrote Mechanic scripts to clean up incoming order reports to fit Vitro's standard nomenclature, and to also alert Vitro staff of the ordering of specific types of items.

The updates to the website allowed for Vitro to start running analyses on the data that they have gathered so far, and past data that we were able to access and clean up for them. This enabled Vitro staff to more accurately analyze metrics such as what popular products and samples are being ordered, which companies they are being shipped to, etc.

We also completed the development of two dashboards that would allow Vitro to track and calculate different OTIF (On Time and In Full), orders, and inventory metrics. Vitro is a company that is in the midst of digitization, and the dashboards combined with the website updates unlocked new datapoints and analyses that would enable Vitro to make better decisions within the market for their products.

Wellness app prototype interface

We prototyped a wellness app for a company that is trying to transition into the wellness space. I programmed the app in a low-code framework called Bubble, with assets created by our visual design team. The app had an API connection to Google Health which allowed for us to demo a functioning prototype that could display simple metrics about the user's health. Our demo primarily focused on the on-boarding process, and then screens that could be looked at in tandem with a user journey.

Coca-Cola training materials redesign

Assisted during the redesign and creation of training materials for Coca-Cola staff. We designed, tested, and then wireframed new training materials and SharePoint websites that were shipped to Coca-Cola for integration in their internal operations. The materials consisted of Figma-based interactive guides, voice-overs for training videos, and documents that could be accessed to learn about one of Coca-Cola's internal management systems. I helped with recording voice-overs and creating and handing off wireframes to Coca-Cola's team.

VueReal Micro-LED technology assessment

Helped an architectural glass company with technological verification and roadmapping for a new investment into a Micro-LED company named VueReal, and how their Micro-LED technology could be integrated into existing and future projects. I helped with research into how this technology could be used in the short, medium, and long-term technological horizons within various industries such as construction, automotive, or healthcare.
