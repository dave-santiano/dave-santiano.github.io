About me

Hi! I am David Santiano, and I am currently working as a fellow at New York University Shanghai's Interactive Media Arts department. Right now, I am exploring and learning different ways to remix, hack, and break various emerging technologies in order to assist and inspire. I am also interested in a wide variety of other disciplines in the visual arts, including photography, animation, and 3D modeling. Aurally, I play guitar, bass, and produce a bit of my own music on the side. My current works in progress involve works that sit at the cross-roads of the auditory, visual, and emerging arts, a little preview of what's to come.
