About me

Hi! My name is David Santiano and I was previously working as a research associate for telepresence at New York University Shanghai's Interactive Media Arts department. Along with that I helped teach a class called VR/AR Fundamentals, where students learned about the fundamentals of immersive media. Now, most of my time is taken up by scrappy, artsy, and explorative research and experimentation. I also make and produce music on the side. If you want to contact me in any way please feel free to reach out.
