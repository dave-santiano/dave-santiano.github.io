About me

>>> Background

Hi! My name is David Santiano. I was working for a design/strategy/engineering firm named propelland as their in-house design technologist, and before that as a research associate for telepresence at New York University Shanghai's Interactive Media Arts department. Along with that I helped teach a class called VR/AR Fundamentals, where students learned about the fundamentals of immersive media.

Now, most of my time is taken up by scrappy, artsy, and explorative research and experimentation. I also make and produce music on the side. If you want to contact me in any way please feel free to reach out.
